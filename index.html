On the weekend
Of November 2024,
I went on a short trip
to Ithaca, NY
To visit my brother.
A five-hour bus trip,
That was quite a hassle to get to,
Was nothing short of a mesmerizing journey.
While on the journey, I got a text from my brother
What do you want for dinner?
Do you want to make biryani?
Ok, I'll head out to get stuff.
The sun was setting as I reached.
We reached home and my brother started preparing dinner.
